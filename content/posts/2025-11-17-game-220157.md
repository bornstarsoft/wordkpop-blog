In an exciting announcement, Game Pop Studio has revealed their upcoming title, "They Will Come," a steampunk horror adventure puzzle game set to launch on PC via Steam in 2026. This game promises to immerse players in a richly detailed alternate history world, combining unique visuals with a bittersweet narrative that is sure to captivate fans of the genre.

Players will take on the role of Little Benjamin, armed with a trusty slingshot and electrobanger, as he navigates a treacherous environment filled with towering automatons and the ominous Wretched Ones. The gameplay focuses on exploration, item collection, and puzzle-solving, all while maintaining a stealthy approach to avoid detection. With a family in peril and a world teeming with danger, the stakes couldn't be higher.

As we look forward to the release of "They Will Come," it raises the question: how will this game redefine the steampunk horror genre? Stay tuned for more updates as we delve deeper into this intriguing title!
