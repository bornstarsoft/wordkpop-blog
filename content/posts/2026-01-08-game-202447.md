The recently launched game AiliA invites players into a captivating realm where mirrors are not just reflections but gateways to intricate puzzles and hidden narratives. Developed by IceToad Studio LLC and published by Gamirror Games, this 3D puzzle adventure is now available on Steam for $11.99, with a limited-time 20% discount during its launch period.

In AiliA, players step into the shoes of Aria, a young girl navigating through beautifully crafted stages that blur the line between reality and a mirrored world. The games unique mechanics allow players to manipulate mirrors to alter their environment, revealing new paths and solutions to over 100 puzzles. With its engaging gameplay and stunning visuals, AiliA promises to challenge both your intellect and imagination.

For those eager to dive into this reflective journey, a free demo is available on Steam, allowing players to experience the magic of AiliA firsthand. As we look to the future of puzzle games, AiliA stands out as a testament to innovative design and storytelling. Will you uncover the secrets hidden within the reflections?
