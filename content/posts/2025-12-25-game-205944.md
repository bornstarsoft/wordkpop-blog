The excitement is building as BeXide announces that the multiplayer party game, Fruit Mountain Party, will be making its way to the Switch 2 and the original Switch in Spring 2026. This vibrant 3D puzzle game, which has been in Early Access on PC since August 2025, promises to deliver a unique blend of fun and competition, allowing players to engage in fruit battles and combine fruits for bigger scores.

Players can look forward to heated online battles for up to four players, as well as a single-player mode that supports global rankings. The games mechanics are designed to be easy to pick up, making it accessible for gamers of all ages. With events like the Osaka Game Dungeon and Tokyo eSports Festa showcasing the Switch version, the anticipation for its release is palpable.

As we await the launch, one cant help but wonder how this game will evolve on the new hardware and what new features might be introduced. Will you be ready to stack those fruits and compete with friends?
