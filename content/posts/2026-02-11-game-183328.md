The February 2026 Steam Next Fest is just around the corner, running from February 23rd to March 2nd, and it promises an array of exciting game demos that every gaming enthusiast should check out. With a variety of genres represented, from cozy farming adventures to intense horror experiences, theres something for everyone. Notably, many of these demos are already available, giving players a sneak peek into the innovative worlds developers have crafted.

As the festival approaches, keep an eye out for updates on additional demos that may catch your interest.
