The upcoming pixel art block-pushing puzzle game, Hazard Pay, is generating buzz in the gaming community as it prepares for its launch on PlayStation 5, Switch, and PC this June. Developed by Smitner Studio and published by NumskulL Games, the game promises a unique blend of stealth, survival horror, and narrative choices, all set against the backdrop of a chilling Cold War-era research facility.

Set in 1982, players take on the role of a government cleanup specialist tasked with navigating a facility rife with biological horrors resulting from failed experiments. The games handcrafted puzzle levels challenge players to erase evidence and make critical decisions that could alter the fate of trapped scientists and the outcome of the narrative. With its engaging mechanics and environmental storytelling, Hazard Pay aims to captivate players seeking both challenge and depth in their gaming experience.

As we await its release, one has to wonder: will Hazard Pay redefine the puzzle genre with its innovative approach, or will it become just another title in the crowded gaming landscape? Only time will tell.
