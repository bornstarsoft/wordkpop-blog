Exciting news for gamers everywhere! BeXide has officially announced the full release of their highly anticipated game, Fruit Mountain Party, set to launch on April 23rd. After a successful early access phase, the developers have taken player feedback to heart, focusing on enhancing gameplay stability and overall experience. This anime-styled 3D puzzle game promises to bring a fresh twist to the beloved Fruit Mountain franchise, now transformed into a chaotic party battle experience.

Fruit Mountain Party invites players to merge fruits and compete against friends in various game modes, whether online or locally. With up to four players able to join in on the fun, the game is designed for laughter and friendly competition. The addition of new features, including improved frame rates on the Nintendo Switch 2, ensures that players will enjoy a smoother and more engaging gameplay experience. Whether you're a seasoned gamer or new to the genre, this game is set to deliver a delightful mix of strategy and fun.

As we look forward to its release, one can't help but wonder: will Fruit Mountain Party become the go-to game for gatherings and online play? Only time will tell, but one thing is for sureit's shaping up to be a must-have for puzzle game enthusiasts.
