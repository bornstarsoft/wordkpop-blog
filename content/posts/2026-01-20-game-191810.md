If youre someone who finds joy in tidying up and creating aesthetically pleasing spaces, then the upcoming game Hozy might just be your perfect match. This delightful sandbox puzzle game combines elements of popular titles like Unpacking and PowerWash Simulator, allowing players to clean and decorate various rooms at their own pace. With its charming graphics and satisfying gameplay, Hozy is designed to cater to those who crave order and creativity in their gaming experience.

In Hozy, players will tackle levels that require them to clean up clutter and then unleash their inner interior designer. The freedom to choose where to place furniture and decorations adds a layer of creativity that many players will appreciate. Whether youre arranging a cozy living room or a compact studio apartment, the game encourages experimentation without the pressure of right or wrong placements.

While the game is still awaiting a release date, the excitement surrounding it is palpable. As we look forward to 2026, one can only wonder how Hozy will inspire players to embrace their neat-freak tendencies while providing a relaxing escape.
