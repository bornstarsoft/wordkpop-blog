Merge Donuts, a delightful puzzle game, is inviting pet owners to showcase their furry friends in its first-ever Furry Friends fan contest. This unique opportunity allows players to submit photos or short videos of their pets for a chance to see them transformed into collectible donut characters within the game. With the contest running from February 18 to March 4, now is the time to grab your best pet pic and join in the fun!

To participate, simply post a picture or video of your pet on Instagram or TikTok, tag the games official accounts, and use the hashtags #MyMergeDonutPet and #MergeDonuts. Up to 12 winners will be selected based on creativity and charm, with their pets immortalized in the game. This contest not only celebrates pets but also engages the gaming community in a fun and interactive way.

As the gaming landscape continues to evolve, contests like these bridge the gap between real-life experiences and virtual worlds, creating memorable moments for players. Will you take the plunge and enter your pet for a chance at donut immortality?
