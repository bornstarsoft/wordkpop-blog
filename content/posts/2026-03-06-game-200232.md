In a remarkable achievement, Battlefield 6 has been crowned the UK Game of the Year at the UKIE Video Game Awards 2026, alongside winning the title of Best PC Game. This first-person shooter, developed by Battlefield Studios with contributions from Criterion Games, shattered records by selling over seven million copies within just three days of its launch, making it the top-selling game of 2025 in the American market.

The UKIE Awards highlighted not only Battlefield 6's success but also the diversity of talent in the gaming industry, with Atomfall taking home the Best UK Console Game award and PowerWash Simulator recognized as the Best UK Mobile Game. The event also saw significant figures inducted into the UK Games Industry Hall of Fame, showcasing the rich history and future potential of gaming in the UK.
