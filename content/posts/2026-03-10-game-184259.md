If youre a fan of cozy games and adorable cats, then Kitty Haven is a title you wont want to miss! This upcoming puzzle game invites players to create the perfect sanctuary for their furry friends, each with their unique personalities and quirks. Whether its accommodating a picky eater or resolving cat squabbles, the game challenges you to arrange spaces thoughtfully, ensuring that every little furball feels at home.

Kitty Haven not only allows you to unlock items and new residents but also encourages creativity as you decorate and solve puzzles to enhance your haven. Available on PC with a demo currently on Steam, this game promises to deliver a delightful experience for both casual gamers and cat enthusiasts alike. As we look forward to its full release, one has to wonder: how will you design your ideal kitty sanctuary?
