Nintendo Switch 2 owners can now rejoice as one of the best games of 2025, Blue Prince, has been released without any prior announcement. This captivating puzzle game, developed by Dogubomb, combines roguelike elements with an intriguing storyline set in an old manor, making it a must-play for fans of the genre. With an impressive Metacritic score of 92/100, Blue Prince has quickly established itself as a standout title, joining the ranks of legendary puzzle games like Portal and Braid.

The release of Blue Prince comes at a time when the Nintendo Switch 2 has already seen a strong lineup of games, including Mario Kart World and Hades 2. However, many third-party titles have yet to make their way to the platform. Fortunately, Blue Prince is now available on the eShop at a special launch price of $19.79, valid until March 12th. This limited-time offer makes it an excellent opportunity for puzzle enthusiasts to dive into one of the most acclaimed games of the year.

As we look forward to more exciting titles on the Nintendo Switch 2, Blue Prince sets a high bar for future releases. Will this game inspire more developers to bring their best works to the platform? Only time will tell.
