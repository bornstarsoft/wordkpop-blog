The release of iRacing's NASCAR 25 Console Game marks a significant moment for fans of NASCAR gaming, especially with its impressive array of base paint schemes. This latest installment not only revives the excitement of console NASCAR gaming but also introduces a diverse selection of paint schemes from various drivers across the three NASCAR National Series, including the much-anticipated ARCA Menards Series.

While some fan-favorite paint schemes and sponsors are notably absentlike those from Monster Energy and certain alcoholic beverage brandsthe game still manages to deliver a nostalgic experience. The inclusion of iconic sponsors such as Red Bull, making its return since NASCAR The Game: 2011, adds to the thrill for long-time fans. This blend of new and familiar elements creates a rich tapestry for players to explore and enjoy.
