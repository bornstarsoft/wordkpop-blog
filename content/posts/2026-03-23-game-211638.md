Exciting news for retro gaming fans! Konami is set to release its own Picross game, titled **Picross S: Konami Antiques Collection**, on April 30 for the Nintendo Switch. This new addition to the Picross series will feature 585 puzzles inspired by over 80 classic Konami titles, including beloved franchises like **Castlevania**, **Metal Gear**, and **Contra**. Players will engage in the familiar puzzle-solving mechanics that have made Picross a staple in the gaming community, revealing pixel art sprites as they progress.

The game promises a diverse range of puzzles, including standard Picross, Mega Picross, and even Color Picross challenges, ensuring that both newcomers and seasoned players will find something to enjoy. With its nostalgic nod to Konami's rich gaming history, this release not only celebrates the past but also invites a new generation of gamers to experience the charm of classic pixel art.

As the Picross series continues to expand, it raises the question: what other beloved franchises could benefit from a similar treatment in the future? Stay tuned for more updates as we count down to the release date!
