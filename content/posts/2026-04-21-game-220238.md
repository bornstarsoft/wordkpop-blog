Sanrio, the beloved creator of Hello Kitty, is stepping into the gaming arena with its new brand, Sanrio Games. This strategic move aims to tap into the fast-growing global gaming market by launching a series of party games for Nintendo Switch and Switch 2 formats. With plans to release ten titles over the next three years, including a highly anticipated console game this fall, Sanrio is poised to expand its character universe into interactive entertainment, a trend that many Japanese IP owners are following.

The companys CEO, Tomokuni Tsuji, emphasized the potential of gaming to enhance their licensing business, particularly in the North American market. By allowing players to create avatars in a town filled with Sanrio characters, they are not just selling games; they are creating immersive experiences that could resonate with fans worldwide. However, the road ahead may be challenging, as the standalone gaming business is expected to incur losses initially, with the success hinging on the ability to leverage their iconic characters effectively.

As Sanrio prepares for its Hollywood debut with a Hello Kitty movie set for 2028, one can't help but wonder: will their foray into gaming redefine how we interact with these beloved characters? The future looks bright, but only time will tell if they can capture the hearts of gamers as they have with fans around the globe.
